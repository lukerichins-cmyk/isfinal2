The vision behind the trip

This version of the site turns the itinerary into something a little more editorial: less checklist, more atmosphere. It keeps the route from the main page, but leans into the landscapes, long train rides, memorable stops, and the overall feeling of spending spring in Spain and France.

Asturias, Spain

The first stretch of the trip is all about northern Spain: dramatic ridgelines, coastal air, river swims, and the kind of scenery that makes every detour feel worth it.

France Finale

The France portion brings a different energy: lakeside beauty, historic cities, and a slower rhythm before the final flight home out of Marseille.

Route and rhythm

From Salt Lake to Madrid, through Asturias, onward to Lyon and Marseille, the route mixes structure with enough flexibility to leave room for the best unexpected moments.
